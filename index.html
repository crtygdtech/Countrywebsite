China it’s located in East Asia with a population exceeding 1.4 billion, being the second-most populous country after India, which represents 17% of the world’s population. China’s capital is Beijing, the president of China is Xi Jinping and its official language is Mandarin, Putonghua.

China is famous for its diverse culinary traditions such as the dishes Dim Sum, Peking Duck, and Kung Pao chicken.
